## 1. PSL University (Paris Sciences & Lettres) — France, QS #28

### Relevant Master's Programs

**MSc Artificial Intelligence and Society** (OUTSTANDING FIT)
- Interdisciplinary program combining AI with social sciences, ethics, and policy
- Pathways include "Governance and societal impact of AI"
- Courses: AI law, data security & privacy, geopolitics of AI, ethics and responsibility in AI
- Career outcomes: AI Ethics & Governance Specialist, Policy Advisor, Computational Social Scientist
- Application: January 15 - February 24, 2026
- Website: https://psl.eu/en/education/master-s-degree-artificial-intelligence-and-society

**Master Sustainability Science**
- Combines disciplinary expertise with inter/transdisciplinary approaches for ecological and social transitions
- Project-based learning with researchers and non-academic stakeholders
- Website: https://psl.eu/en/education/master-sustainability-science

### Scholarship Options

| Scholarship | Coverage | Full-Ride? | Brazilian Eligible? | Deadline |
|------------|----------|-----------|-------------------|----------|
| **France Excellence Eiffel Scholarship** | €1,200/month stipend + international transport + insurance + housing assistance. **Does NOT cover tuition fees.** | NO (no tuition) | YES | ~November (institution nominates; cannot apply directly) |
| **Dauphine-PSL / Fondation Polytechnique Scholarships** | Partial to full tuition fee waivers; varies by program | PARTIAL | YES (automatic consideration upon admission) | Varies by program |
| **CROUS Social Grants** | Based on financial need; living allowance | NO | YES (if residing in France) | Varies |

**Assessment:** PSL has the single best-fit program (AI and Society) but lacks a clear full-ride scholarship for non-EU students. The Eiffel scholarship is strong but does not cover tuition. Best combined with external funding (e.g., Fundacao Estudar, CAPES).

## 2. King's College London (KCL) — United Kingdom, QS #31

### Relevant Master's Programs

**MSc Artificial Intelligence** (GOOD FIT)
- Includes ethics and philosophy of AI, social/legal/professional dimensions
- Website: https://www.kcl.ac.uk/study/postgraduate-taught/courses/artificial-intelligence-msc

**Artificial Intelligence in Public Policy** (module, not full master's)
- Covers governance and applicability of AI in public policy

**MSc Digital Humanities** (PARTIAL FIT)
- Explores the evolving role of digital technologies in society

Note: KCL does not have a dedicated "AI Ethics/Governance" master's like Edinburgh. The MSc AI covers ethics as a component.

### Scholarship Options

| Scholarship | Coverage | Full-Ride? | Brazilian Eligible? | Deadline |
|------------|----------|-----------|-------------------|----------|
| **Chevening Scholarship (UK Government)** | Full tuition + monthly living allowance (~£1,652/month in London) + flights + visa | YES | YES (Brazil is Chevening-eligible) | Applications closed for 2026-27; next cycle opens August 2026 for 2027-28 |
| **CMA CGM Excellence Fund** | Tuition fee waiver + stipend of £22,161 | YES | Likely (check eligibility) | April 24, 2026 |
| **Commonwealth Scholarships** | Full tuition + living allowance (£1,378-1,690/month) + clothing allowance | YES | NO (Commonwealth countries only; Brazil is not a member) |
| **KCL Department Scholarships** | Varies; typically partial tuition | NO | YES | Varies by department |

**Assessment:** Chevening is the best path for a Brazilian at KCL -- fully funded, and Brazil is eligible. However, the 2026-27 cycle is closed. The CMA CGM Excellence Fund is worth investigating. KCL's AI program includes ethics but is more technically focused than Edinburgh's dedicated AI Ethics MSc.

## 3. University of Edinburgh — United Kingdom, QS #34

### Relevant Master's Programs

**MSc Data and Artificial Intelligence Ethics** (OUTSTANDING FIT)
- Housed at the Edinburgh Futures Institute, Centre for Technomoral Futures
- Draws on philosophy, law, informatics, and science & technology innovation studies
- Focus: ethical design, use, and governance of AI and data-intensive technologies
- Ranked 4th globally for sustainability (QS 2026 Sustainability Rankings)
- Learning outcomes include AI governance, responsible innovation, stakeholder engagement
- NOTE: Part-time/online/PgCert/PgDip pathways paused; full-time on-campus MSc open
- Website: https://study.ed.ac.uk/programmes/postgraduate-taught/1092-data-and-artificial-intelligence-ethics

| Scholarship | Coverage | Full-Ride? | Brazilian Eligible? | Deadline |

| **Burdett Fellows Studentship** | Full tuition + stipend | YES | Check specific eligibility | Varies |

## 5. Institut Polytechnique de Paris — France, QS #41

### Relevant Master's Programs

**MSc Data Science & AI for Business (X-HEC)** (PARTIAL FIT)
- Joint program with HEC Paris; ranked #2 worldwide (QS 2026)
- Business-oriented AI, not specifically ethics/governance focused

**DataAI Study Track** (PARTIAL FIT)
- 2-year master's in AI and large-scale data management
- More technical than policy-focused

**MSc Environmental Engineering and Sustainability Management** (PARTIAL FIT)
- Sustainability focus but not AI-specific
- Website: https://programmes.polytechnique.edu/en/master-all-msct-programs/environmental-engineering-and-sustainability-management

**Hi! PARIS Center research** (not a degree program)
- Collaboration between IP Paris, HEC Paris, and Inria Saclay
- Addresses societal implications: sustainability, ethics, law, and regulation of AI

Note: IP Paris is strong in technical AI and sustainability engineering, but lacks a dedicated AI ethics/governance/policy master's. The AI+society angle lives in research centers, not in a specific degree program.

## 6. Delft University of Technology (TU Delft) — Netherlands, QS #47

### Relevant Master's Programs

**MSc Engineering and Policy Analysis (EPA)** (EXCELLENT FIT)
- 2-year program at the intersection of engineering and policymaking
- Focus on complex socio-technical problems, modeling for policymaking
- Year 1 taught in The Hague; Year 2 partly in Delft
- Career outcomes: strategic advisors to governments, regulatory bodies, international organizations
- 90% of graduates employed within 3 months
- Website: https://www.tudelft.nl/en/education/programmes/masters/epa/msc-engineering-and-policy-analysis

**TPM AI Elective Package** (can supplement any TU Delft master's)
- Courses: Socio-technical Perspective on AI Governance & Institutional Design, Ethics of AI, Robots and Society
- Open to all TU Delft master's students
- Website: https://www.tudelft.nl/en/tpm/education/tpm-ai-elective-package

**AI & Society course (IDE)** (elective)
- Elective course on artificial intelligence and society

**TU Delft institutional strengths:**
- WHO Collaborating Center on AI for Health Governance
- Delft Design for Values initiative (AI as application area)
- Research theme: "Responsible Design and Engineering of Human-centered AI and Data Driven Systems"

### Scholarship Options

| Scholarship | Coverage | Full-Ride? | Brazilian Eligible? | Deadline |
|------------|----------|-----------|-------------------|----------|
| **Justus & Louise van Effen Excellence Scholarships** | Full tuition (€30,000/yr for non-EU) + living expenses contribution + TU Delft Scholarship Club membership | YES | YES (confirmed: past Brazilian recipient from UFMS) | December 1, 2025 (CLOSED for 2026-27) |
| **Holland Scholarship** | €5,000 one-time | NO | YES (non-EEA students) | Varies |
| **Orange Knowledge Programme** (Nuffic)** | Tuition + living + travel | YES | Check country eligibility | Varies |

**Assessment:** TU Delft's EPA program is an excellent fit -- engineering + policy analysis, with AI governance electives available. The van Effen scholarship is genuinely full-ride and has accepted Brazilians before. However, the 2026-27 deadline has passed. For the 2027-28 cycle, deadline would likely be December 1, 2026.

## Summary Ranking by Fit

### Best Program Fit (AI + Ethics/Governance/Social Impact):
1. **University of Edinburgh** — MSc Data and AI Ethics (purpose-built for this exact intersection)
2. **PSL University** — MSc AI and Society (outstanding interdisciplinary program)
3. **TU Delft** — MSc Engineering and Policy Analysis + AI governance electives
4. **King's College London** — MSc AI (ethics as component, not primary focus)
5. **Institut Polytechnique de Paris** — Technical AI + sustainability engineering
6. **University of Manchester** — MSc Sustainable Business / Environmental Governance (no dedicated AI ethics program)

### Best Scholarship Accessibility for Brazilians:
1. **TU Delft** — van Effen (confirmed Brazilian recipients; genuine full-ride; next cycle Dec 2026)
2. **UK Universities (Edinburgh, KCL, Manchester)** — via Chevening (full-ride; Brazil eligible; next cycle Aug 2026)
3. **PSL / IP Paris** — Eiffel (stipend only, no tuition) + institutional scholarships (partial to near-full)

### Top Recommendation:
**University of Edinburgh MSc Data and AI Ethics + Chevening Scholarship** is the strongest combination of program fit and funding for a Brazilian engineer with 10+ years in tech/AI interested in AI ethics, governance, and social impact. Apply for Chevening in August 2026 for the 2027-28 academic year.

**TU Delft MSc Engineering and Policy Analysis + van Effen Scholarship** is the second-best option, with a confirmed track record of funding Brazilians. Apply by December 1, 2026 for the 2027-28 intake.

## Cross-Cutting Scholarship: Chevening (applies to all 3 UK universities)

- **Funder:** UK Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office
- **Coverage:** Full tuition + monthly living (£1,334 outside London / £1,652 in London) + return flights + visa + allowances
- **Brazilian eligible:** YES (Brazil has a dedicated Chevening page: https://www.chevening.org/scholarship/brazil/)
- **Requirements:** 2+ years work experience, undergraduate degree, commit to return home for 2 years post-study
- **Next cycle:** Opens August 2026 for 2027-28 intake
- **Website:** https://www.chevening.org/scholarships/
