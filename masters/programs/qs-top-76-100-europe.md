# QS World University Rankings 2026: European Universities Ranked 76-100
## Full-Ride Scholarships & Relevant Master's Programs for a Brazilian Engineer

Research date: March 2026

---

## Summary

Of the 25 universities ranked 76-100 in the QS World University Rankings 2026, **14 are in Europe**. After thorough research, **truly full-ride scholarships (tuition + living costs)** from the universities themselves are rare. The best full-ride opportunities come from **external schemes** (Chevening, DAAD EPOS, Commonwealth, Swiss Government Excellence) that can be applied to these universities. A few universities do offer their own full-ride or near-full-ride packages.

### Top Picks (strongest combination of relevant programs + funding)

1. **University of Birmingham** (UK) — MSc AI and Government / MSc AI and Sustainable Development + Chevening
2. **KTH Royal Institute of Technology** (Sweden) — MSc Sustainable Digitalisation + KTH Scholarship (tuition only) + SI Scholarship
3. **Politecnico di Milano** (Italy) — Master AI for Public Services (AI4Gov) + Merit Scholarships
4. **University of Glasgow** (UK) — LLM with AI & Law modules + Chevening
5. **University of Southampton** (UK) — MA AI (Digital Transformation) / MSc Management & AI + Chevening

## 2. KTH Royal Institute of Technology (Sweden) — QS #78

### Relevant Master's Programs
- **MSc Sustainable Digitalisation** — Prepares students to tackle sustainability challenges using digital technologies. Topics: permacomputing, regenerative design, more-than-human worlds, post-colonialism. Leadership in complex change processes.
- **MSc Machine Learning** — Promotes "AI for good" use cases (wind/solar efficiency, disease diagnosis, precision farming). Integrates UN SDGs.
- **MSc Sustainable Technology** — Industrial Ecology: interaction of technical, economic, social, and ecological systems.

### Scholarships
| Scholarship | Coverage | Full-ride? | Eligibility | Deadline |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| KTH Scholarship | Full tuition fee waiver | Tuition only | Non-EU/EEA citizens, merit-based. 8% acceptance rate (65/803 in 2025) | 15 Jan 2026 (closed) |
| **Swedish Institute Scholarship for Global Professionals (external)** | **Full tuition + living (SEK 10,000/mo) + travel + insurance** | **YES** | **Brazilians eligible** | **~Feb each year** |

### Verdict
**EXCELLENT fit.** MSc Sustainable Digitalisation is a standout program. KTH Scholarship covers tuition only. The Swedish Institute Scholarship (SISGP) is the full-ride path — Brazilians are eligible. Apply for autumn 2027.
